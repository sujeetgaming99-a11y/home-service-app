FAQ

How do I track my worker?

Open the Track page after confirming a booking. Your latest active booking will appear there.

Can I cancel a booking?

Yes. You can cancel from the Booking page or the Worker Tracking page before completion.

Where is my booking history saved?

Bookings are saved locally in your browser using localStorage until a backend is added.

How do I contact my worker?

Use the Call Worker button on tracking or worker profile pages.
